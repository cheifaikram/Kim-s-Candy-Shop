About Us

Why Would You Choose Us?

When COVID hit, most of my family members lost their jobs, so we thought of building a small business to go through, and since all of us were passionate about sweets and candy, mostly chocolate, we decided to open a candy shop in our garage. Everyone in our town was ordering, and word spread throughout England and eventually around the world. We pack our sweets with love, and we always include some extra candy or gifts with the order, as well as free delivery to South African countries because they are the biggest consumers.
